Baingan Bharta

Description

Baingan Bharta is a smoky-flavored dish from Punjab made with roasted eggplant, onions, tomatoes, and aromatic spices. It is typically served with roti or naan.

The eggplant is roasted over an open flame, giving it a unique smoky flavor, then mashed and cooked with flavorful spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Steps

  1. Roast the eggplant over an open flame until the skin is charred and the inside is soft.
  2. Let it cool, then peel off the skin and mash the flesh.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies, and saut√© until onions turn golden.
  5. Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes turn mushy.
  6. Mix in the mashed eggplant and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Stir in garam masala and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or naan.
