Dhokla

Description

Dhokla is a soft and spongy steamed snack from Gujarat made with gram flour (besan). It is light, fluffy, and has a slightly tangy taste, perfect as a breakfast or evening snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp eno fruit salt (or baking soda)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup water for steaming
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Mix gram flour, yogurt, water, salt, sugar, and turmeric in a bowl to form a smooth batter. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Add eno fruit salt, mix well, and immediately pour the batter into a greased steaming tray.
  3. Steam for 15-20 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  4. Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chili. Pour this tempering over the steamed dhokla.
  5. Cut into pieces, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve with green chutney.
